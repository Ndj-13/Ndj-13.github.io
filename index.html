About

Hi! I'm a generalist artist, specialized in 2D and 3D textures and character design, currently located in Madrid. Currently, I'm working on my Final Degree Project titled After the Storm, a short film that combines 3D animation with 2D effects, featuring a beautiful and emotional story. In my free time, I enjoy creating my own illustrations, going for a run, and spending time with family and friends. Thank you for visiting my site! Feel free to contact me, it’s always a pleasure to discover new people!.

Endless Dream

Endless Dream is a puzzle videogame developed by a group of students from my degree. We had less than 3 months for it to be able to play on platforms so it was a real challenge. Personally, I had the pleasure of designing one of the worlds, inspired in the dream of counting sheeps while we sleep, with a simple hand-made style, designed to be visually appealing and enhace inmersion through it's beauty.
